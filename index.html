DTUSEM Ltd. now has new products available to all clients....

After a difficult year for all, DTUSEM are happy to announce that we are now able to provide all DTUSEM clients three new types of insurance to keep yourselves or your business insured to the highest level.

New insurance products available, to new clients, and clients with existing policies:

Legal Expenses Insurance

We have worked in association with the Legal Expenses Insurance provider'ARAG' to be able to offer our clients two types of Legal Expenses Cover options to would benefit our clients:

- Including Contract & Debt
- Excluding Contract & Debt

After receiving feedback from a multitued of DTUSEM clients, ARAG has created two types of cover to choose from in order to best cover yourself indivudally when building your tailor made policy.

Cyber Insurance

In the world of Internet and technology, purchasing a Cyber policy is a fantastic way to keep yourself cover in the event of a cyber attack.

Unfortunately there are increasing cases of cyber theft (for example Invoice Hijacking or Social Engineering Scams), and with cover for 110,000 E-Theft, Social Engineering, Loss of Data, Un-Planned System Outages, damage to reputation via Social Media as well as many others, Cyber is a hot-topic policy.

Home Office Insurances

DTUSEM Risk Management are now able to offer all clients our'Home Office Insurance' options:

  • - Home Office Contents (Up to £5,000)
  • - Portable Equipment (Up to £5,000)
  • - Home Office Computer Equipment (Up to £5,000)


This has been a popular add-on for clients before, and we have now expanded this to all. These cover options allow you to protect your home offices and equipment, especially when many of us are working from home!

If you are a current client with a policy in place, you can easily add any of the above types of cover to your current policy by logging in to your My DTUSEM Portal and clicking the link 'Change My Cover'

Premium Funding payment option is now available!

2020 has been a difficult year for all, so we have now setup a monthly payment option to help clients pay for their policies at renewal time, without the worry of having to pay for a year upfront in one payment.

Clients now have the option to pay monthly without having to purposely reduce their level of cover / remove types of cover / add new types of cover that you would like, in order to keep costs down.

There are now three payment options:

  • - Premium Monthly Funding
  • - Card Payment (both credit and debit cards accepted)
  • - Bank Transfer (BACs)
*Our monthly premium option is available via premium finance through Orchard Funding Limited, at an interest rate of 23.23% (APR). Furthermore, a credit check will be carried out during the application process.
